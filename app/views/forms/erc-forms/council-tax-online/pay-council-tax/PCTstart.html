Pay council tax

Set up a direct debit or find out other ways you can pay your council tax.

Direct debit

The easiest way to pay your council tax is by direct debit. You can pay in 10 monthly instalments and choose the date you'd like to pay.

Call 0141 577 3002 to set up a direct debit over the phone.

 Download Direct Debit application form [243kb]

Pay online by card

You'll need your council tax reference number.

Automated telephone line

Call 0141 577 3030 to pay by card. You'll need your council tax reference number.

Post office or PayPoint

Pay at any post office or PayPoint using the barcode on your bill.

Find your nearest PayPoint store

Post or in person

Make cheques payable to East Renfrewshire Council. Make sure you write your property address and reference number on the back.

Your payment should reach us by the first day of every month.

If you come into council offices, opening times are 9.00am until 4.15pm, Monday to Friday

Between 1.00pm and 2.00pm, only debit or credit card payments can be taken.
